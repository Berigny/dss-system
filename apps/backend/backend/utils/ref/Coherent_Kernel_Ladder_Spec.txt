\section{Coherent Kernel Ladder}

This is a reconstruction of the $0$--$9$ ladder as a single, coherent mathematical narrative, borrowing the \emph{cleanest} framing from:

\begin{itemize}[nosep]
  \item harmonic / standing-wave structure (eigenmodes, closure, integer labels),
  \item wave-domain physics (6D field-state, invariants, null vs non-null, phase field $\Theta$),
  \item the dual-substrate memory idea (continuous inference + discrete, hierarchical ledger memory).
\end{itemize}

The guiding aesthetic is \textbf{minimal objects, maximal explanatory reach}:

\begin{quote}
What must exist, for anything to persist?

Persistence demands \textbf{closure} (a loop) and \textbf{invariants} (what survives the loop).
\end{quote}

\subsection{The whole ladder in one line}

\textbf{Reality can be read as a phase field forced to close on itself at multiple levels}:

\begin{itemize}[nosep]
  \item geometric closure (standing waves),
  \item topological closure (winding / flux),
  \item dynamical closure (feedback / control),
  \item normative closure (ethics / telos).
\end{itemize}

Each rung is one more kind of closure.

\section{Shared primitives (used across rungs)}

\subsection{The wave-domain ``state'' (6D)}

Treat the electromagnetic configuration as a single point in a six-dimensional space:
\[
X(\mathbf r,t) \equiv \bigl(\mathbf E(\mathbf r,t),\, c\mathbf B(\mathbf r,t)\bigr) \in \mathbb{R}^6.
\]

Energy density and transport:
\[
\nu(\mathbf r,t)=\frac{\varepsilon_0}{2}\bigl(|\mathbf E|^2+c^2|\mathbf B|^2\bigr),\qquad
\mathbf S=\mu_0^{-1} \mathbf E\times \mathbf B.
\]

A compact, dimensionless ``how propagating is it?'' diagnostic:
\[
\tau(\mathbf r,t)\equiv \frac{|\mathbf S|}{\nu c}=\frac{2\,|\mathbf E\times (c\mathbf B)|}{|\mathbf E|^2+c^2|\mathbf B|^2}\in[0,1].
\]

\subsection{Two Lorentz invariants and a phase (dephase)}

\[
I_1 = |\mathbf E|^2-c^2|\mathbf B|^2,\qquad I_2=\mathbf E\cdot \mathbf B.
\]

Package them into a single complex invariant:
\[
I \equiv I_1+2icI_2.
\]

Then the argument of $I$ defines a compact ``mixing''/dephase coordinate:
\[
\varphi_d\equiv -\tfrac12\arg(I).
\]

\subsection{Null vs non-null (light-like vs matter-like)}

\begin{itemize}[nosep]
  \item \textbf{Null} (radiative, photon-like ideal): $I=0$ (max transport; $\tau\approx 1$).
  \item \textbf{Non-null} (confined / standing / matter-like): $I\neq 0$ (stored/reactive energy; $\tau\approx 0$).
\end{itemize}

\subsection{A fundamental phase field}

Introduce a deeper object: a \textbf{phase field} $\Theta(\mathbf r,t)$ whose boundary-conditioned / topologically constrained configurations generate the modes we call ``particles''. A typical topological quantisation statement:
\[
\oint_\gamma \nabla \Theta\cdot d\ell = 2\pi n,\qquad n\in\mathbb{Z}.
\]

\subsection{Dual substrate memory (continuous + discrete)}

Write the agentic system as a product space:
\[
\mathcal{S} \sim \mathbb{R}\times\mathbb{Q}_p.
\]

\begin{itemize}[nosep]
  \item $\mathbb{R}$: continuous inference / wave-like flow.
  \item $\mathbb{Q}_p$: discrete, hierarchical memory (prime-depth ledger) that makes \emph{corrections stick}.
\end{itemize}

\section{The $0$--$9$ ladder as a closure stack}

\subsection{Eq.0 --- The Void (identity without reference)}

\textbf{Elegant framing:} before any ``thing'', there is no distinction, no coordinate origin, no measurement basis.

Minimal maths: the \textbf{trivial structure}

\begin{itemize}[nosep]
  \item one state,
  \item one symmetry (do nothing),
  \item no observable differences.
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Necessity:} to have information, you need \emph{contrast}.

\subsection{Eq.1 --- The First Cut (distinction; the seed of duality)}

\textbf{Elegant framing:} the first act is not ``matter'' but a \textbf{choice of reference}.

Minimal maths: a split into complementary degrees of freedom.

\begin{itemize}[nosep]
  \item In harmonic language: a boundary appears.
  \item In wave language: amplitude vs phase become meaningful.
  \item In dual-substrate language: flow vs ledger.
\end{itemize}

A clean abstraction:
\[
\text{world-state} \approx (\text{continuous dynamics})\oplus(\text{discrete identity / memory}).
\]

\textbf{Necessity:} without a persistent discrete anchor, learning drifts; without a continuous substrate, nothing flows.

\subsection{Eq.2 --- Time (phase progression; the breath operator)}

\textbf{Elegant framing:} time is the parameter of phase change, not a pre-existing container.

Minimal maths: a phase $\theta\in S^1$ and a monotone parameter that orders its evolution.

A tidy operator view (the ``breath'' instinct):
\[
\text{Breath: } \mathbb{Z}/4\mathbb{Z} \to SU(2)
\]
interpretable as a four-mode processing cycle (latent $\to$ reactive $\to$ associative $\to$ analytical) that repeats.

\textbf{Necessity:} once you have distinction, you get alternation; once you have alternation, you get clocking.

\subsection{Eq.3 --- Space (standing waves; closure becomes geometry)}

\textbf{Elegant framing:} geometry is what you get when oscillation is forced to \textbf{close}.

The simplest mathematical move is an eigenproblem:
\[
\nabla^2\psi + k^2\psi = 0\quad \text{with boundary conditions}.
\]

Closure forces $k$ into discrete families $\to$ integer-labelled modes $\to$ harmonics.

\textbf{Necessity:} space is the record of stable nodes and surfaces that persist under cycling.

\subsection{Eq.4 --- Force (EM as invariant structure in 6D)}

\textbf{Elegant framing:} EM is not two fields; it is one state with invariants.

Use $X=(\mathbf E,c\mathbf B)\in\mathbb{R}^6$ plus invariants $(I_1,I_2)$ (or $I$). Then \textbf{force} is read as how boundary conditions steer the field-state through this invariant landscape.

\begin{itemize}[nosep]
  \item ``Polarisation'' is not just projection; it is \textbf{phase-constraint}.
  \item Measurement is an invariant-probe: you hold a magnitude fixed and sweep a mixing coordinate.
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Necessity:} once geometry exists, the next stable layer is what geometry \emph{does} under change: invariant-guided interaction.

\subsection{Eq.5 --- Gravity / Mass (non-nullness; stored energy becomes inertia)}

\textbf{Elegant framing:} gravity appears when a configuration cannot remain purely propagating.

Wave-domain hinge:

\begin{itemize}[nosep]
  \item light-like propagation sits on the \textbf{null condition} $I=0$,
  \item localisation requires a departure from nullness.
\end{itemize}

A clean scalar cue (one example form):
\[
N^2\equiv |\nabla\Theta|^2-\frac{1}{c^2}(\partial_t\Theta)^2.
\]

\begin{itemize}[nosep]
  \item $N=0$: null (radiative).
  \item $N\neq 0$: non-null (confined / matter-like).
\end{itemize}

Interpretation:

\begin{itemize}[nosep]
  \item mass/inertia is the price of maintaining a non-null configuration,
  \item gravity is the large-scale imprint of that stored gradient/stress.
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Necessity:} the moment you allow stored/reactive energy, you allow long-range structural effects.

\subsection{Eq.6 --- The Observer (self-reference; control loops that stabilise)}

\textbf{Elegant framing:} an ``observer'' is a system that carries an internal model used to keep itself coherent.

\begin{quote}
Persistent mismatch + memory pressure + drift $\to$ a closure loop appears.
\end{quote}

Minimal control form:
\[
(s_{t+1},m_{t+1})=F(s_t,m_t,x_t),\qquad \text{commit if }\mathcal{C}(s_t,m_t)\ge\theta.
\]

Where:

\begin{itemize}[nosep]
  \item $s_t$ is the live wave-like state ($\mathbb{R}$-side),
  \item $m_t$ is the correction-persistent ledger ($\mathbb{Q}_p$-side),
  \item $\mathcal{C}$ is a coherence gate (must include hysteresis / stability, not just lexical alignment).
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Necessity:} without Eq.6, Eq.5 configurations decay or drift; Eq.6 is what makes identity persist.

\subsection{Eq.7 --- Coherence (constraint as what makes corrections stick)}

\textbf{Elegant framing:} coherence is not niceness; it is the condition for sustainable improvement.

Define coherence-over-time as a functional:
\[
\mathrm{Coherence}(t)=
\underbrace{\mathrm{Consistency}}_{\text{no contradiction}}+
\underbrace{\mathrm{Traceability}}_{\text{why we believe it}}+
\underbrace{\mathrm{Correction\ Persistence}}_{\text{fixes don't vanish}}-
\underbrace{\mathrm{Drift}}_{\text{unexamined optimisation}}.
\]

This rung is where ``constraint'' lives: constraints, invariants, auditability.

\textbf{Necessity:} observers without constraint become persuasive drift-machines.

\subsection{Eq.8 --- Ethics (multi-agent coherence; constraint becomes choice)}

\textbf{Elegant framing:} ethics is coherence under freedom.

Once a system can choose, it must choose under constraints that protect the larger field:

\begin{itemize}[nosep]
  \item minimise harm,
  \item preserve agency and dignity,
  \item avoid optimisation that breaks long-term coherence.
\end{itemize}

In maths terms: ethics is the introduction of \textbf{non-negotiable boundary conditions} on action-selection.

\textbf{Necessity:} without Eq.8, Eq.6--7 can be used for domination just as easily as for stewardship.

\subsection{Eq.9 --- Telos (meaning as an attractor over time)}

\textbf{Elegant framing:} telos is the global objective that makes the ladder more than a machine.

A clean way to state it:
\[
\text{Choose ends that increase coherence across time and scale.}
\]

Not maximum output, not maximum control, but \textbf{maximum sustainable coherence}.

\textbf{Necessity:} without telos, ethics is arbitrary; with telos, ethics has a direction.

\textbf{Falsifiable hook:} if the substrate has no robust topological obstruction (no stable defect class), then no persistent closures exist; everything becomes gauge/relabelling.

\subsection{Eq6--Eq9: the sovereignty engine as a bridge viability function}

We now define a small set of scalars computed at each candidate transition.

\subsubsection*{(1) EM invariants (continuous)}

Using standard Lorentz invariants:
\[
I_1 := |\mathbf E|^2 - c^2|\mathbf B|^2,
\qquad
I_2 := 2c\,\mathbf E\cdot \mathbf B.

Interpretation:

\begin{itemize}[nosep]

  \item non-null storage/defect regime: otherwise.

\subsubsection*{(2) Topological invariant (defect/winding)}

Let $W$ be a winding / defect charge derived from $\Theta$:
\[
W := \frac{1}{2\pi}\oint_{\gamma} \nabla\Theta \cdot d\ell \in \mathbb Z.
\]

\subsubsection*{(3) Ledger invariant (tamper + replay)}

Define $K$ as a ledger closure invariant made from:

\begin{enumerate}[nosep]
  \item hash-chain integrity
  \[
  H_t := \mathrm{Hash}(H_{t-1}\Vert\mathrm{payload}_t),
  \]
  \item deterministic replay checksum
  \[
  R_t := \mathbb 1\big[\mathrm{Replay}(\text{kernel_origin},\{\mathrm{payload}\}_{\le t}) = s_t\big].
  \]
\end{enumerate}

Then a minimal scalar:
\[
K(t) := R_t\cdot \mathbb 1[H_t\text{ valid}].
\]
So $K\in\{0,1\}$. If $K=0$, the claim is not reproducible.

\subsubsection*{Gate metrics $(L,H,P)$: the contract terms}

L(X_t) := L_{\mathrm{phys}}(t)\cdot L_{\mathrm{top}}(t)\cdot L_{\mathrm{ledger}}(t).

Where:

\begin{itemize}[nosep]

\Delta(t)=|\Delta I_1|+|\Delta I_2|+\eta|\Delta W|.

Define a short-window hysteresis stability score:

H(X_t) := \exp\Big(-\mu\,\mathrm{Var}_{\tau\in[t-w,t]}\Delta(\tau)\Big).

\paragraph{Provenance completeness $P$ (auditability)}
\[
P(X_t) := \mathbb 1[\text{schema complete}]\cdot \mathbb 1[\text{inputs logged}]\cdot \mathbb 1[\text{version pinned}].
\]

\subsubsection*{Eq6 --- Awareness $A$ (recursive self-model improves correction)}

Let the root projection be $C_t = \Pi_{\mathrm{root}}(m_t)$ and an internal model predict $\hat C_{t+1}$. Prediction error:
\[
E_t := d(C_{t+1},\hat C_{t+1}).
\]

Awareness score (normalised improvement):
\[
A_t := \mathrm{clip}_{[0,1]}\Big(1 - \frac{E_t}{E_t^{\mathrm{baseline}}+\epsilon}\Big).

\subsubsection*{Eq7 --- Unity $U$ (invariants survive the $\infty$ gate)}

Define a deviation norm:
\[
D_t := \alpha_1|\Delta I_1|+\alpha_2|\Delta I_2|+\alpha_3|\Delta W|+\alpha_4(1-K).
\]
Then:
\[
U_t := \exp(-D_t).
\]

\subsubsection*{Eq8 --- Ethics $E$ (admissibility at sinks / boundaries)}

Ethics is a hard admissibility filter at sinks $(3,7)$ before bridge release.

Let $\mathcal E$ be an admissibility constraint set. Then:
\[
E_t := \mathbb 1[X_t\in \mathcal E].
\]

\subsubsection*{Eq9 --- Telos $V$ and closure}

\[
V_t := A_t\cdot U_t\cdot E_t.

\mathrm{BridgeAllowed}(t) := \mathbb 1[V_t\ge \theta_V]\cdot \mathbb 1[L_t\ge \theta_L]\cdot \mathbb 1[H_t\ge \theta_H]\cdot \mathbb 1[P_t=1].

def compute_J(state):
    I1 = norm(state.E)**2 - c**2 * norm(state.B)**2

def compute_LHPAUEV(window):
    # L
    dI1, dI2, dW, K = window.delta_I1(), window.delta_I2(), window.delta_W(), window.K_last()
    L_phys = exp(-lam1*abs(dI1) - lam2*abs(dI2))
    L_top  = int(dW in allowed_dW)
    L_ledger = K
    L = L_phys * L_top * L_ledger

    H = exp(-mu*variance(Delta))

    # P
    P = int(schema_complete() and inputs_logged() and version_pinned())

    # A (awareness)
    E_pred = prediction_error_next_root()

    U = exp(-D)

    # E (ethics)
    E = int(state_in_admissible_set())

    # V (telos/viability)
    V = A * U * E

    return L, H, P, A, U, E, V

def bridge_allowed(metrics):
